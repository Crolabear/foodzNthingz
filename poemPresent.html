Poem Project

I picked up this project as a fun exercise to analyze Chinese text in python. I downloaded an online book on a collection of poems written by various poets in the Tang dynasty. I looked at poems instead of other articles because poems are very concise (and I feel like this is someting not a lot of people explored before.)

The data consisted of poems from 900 different authors. After breaking down the poems, we ended up with over 10000 unique words. (reminder to get a count on how how poems from each author, and how many poems all together.)

I looked at the most frequently used words in those poems, and the most frequently used words at the end of each line (because I want to see how many words tend to rhyme). A future project that spins off from this dataset may be: assuming all the poems rhyme, and classify words into different rhyming categories... stay tone. And I looked at individual poets and see what words they tend to use. Lastly, putted poets into different time lines and get a sense on how word usage in poems change over time.

The following histogram shows the count of the 50 most frequently used words.

It looks like the most used term was "no". Some other words on the list were "one", "people","cloud","wine", "why", "moon", "sky" and "flower". It seems like the poets like to talk about nature.
