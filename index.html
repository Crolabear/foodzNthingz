Tyche

The one that decides your fortune.


She was the presiding tutelary deity that governed the fortune and prosperity of a city, its destiny. She is the daughter of Aphrodite and Zeus or Hermes.

Tyche Facts:

  • Greek Name: Tyche
  • Role & Function: being the goddess of fortune and luck
  • Status: Daughter of the Titan gods Oceanus and Tethys or alternatively Aphrodite and Hermes
  • Symbols: The wheel, wings, the sceptre, cornucopia (horn of plenty), ship rudders and the sceptre
  • Gender: Female
  • Roman Counterpart: The Roman equivalent of Tyche was Fortuna
